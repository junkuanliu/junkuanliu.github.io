Junkuan (Benjamin) Liu

I build dependable multimodal and reinforcement learning systems that help agents understand complex user interfaces. I am a Computer Science BSE candidate at the University of Michigan and a research intern at LG AI Research, where I focus on large-scale GUI agent datasets and training pipelines.

I care about reproducible infrastructure, interpretable evaluation, and productizing research so that advanced reasoning systems become useful to real teams.
